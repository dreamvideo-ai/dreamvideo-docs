About DreamVideo AI

DreamVideo AI is a powerful mobile application that transforms your text prompts into stunning AI-generated videos using cutting-edge Luma AI technology. Create professional-quality videos in seconds, right from your phone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create a video?
A: Simply enter a text prompt describing what you want to see (e.g., "A serene sunset over the ocean with waves crashing"), select your video quality, and tap "Generate Video". Your video will be ready in a few minutes.
Q: What video quality options are available?
A: We offer three quality tiers:
  • Standard: Fast generation, lower cost
  • High: Better quality, balanced speed and cost
  • Premium: Highest quality, longer generation time
Q: How long does it take to generate a video?
A: Generation times vary by quality:
  • Standard: 1-3 minutes
  • High: 3-5 minutes
  • Premium: 5-8 minutes
You'll receive a notification when your video is ready.
Q: What are credits and how do they work?
A: Credits are used to generate videos. Each video costs a certain number of credits based on quality. You can purchase credits through pay-as-you-go or get unlimited generations with a subscription.
Q: What's the difference between subscription and pay-as-you-go?
A:
  • Subscription: Unlimited video generations for a monthly fee, best for regular users
  • Pay-as-you-go: Buy credits as needed, no recurring charges, great for occasional use
Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Go to Settings → Manage Subscription. Or cancel through your Apple App Store or Google Play Store subscription settings. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
Q: Can I download my videos?
A: Yes! All your generated videos are saved in the "My Creations" tab and can be downloaded to your device or shared directly to social media.
Q: Why did my video generation fail?
A: Videos may fail due to:
  • Network connectivity issues - check your internet connection
  • Inappropriate content in prompt - ensure prompts follow our guidelines
  • Server issues - try again in a few minutes
If problems persist, contact support with your video ID.
Q: What happens to my data if I delete my account?
A: When you delete your account (Settings → Delete Account), all your personal data, videos, and account information are permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.
Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and secure cloud storage (Firebase) to protect your data. We never sell your information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Q: Can I use Sign in with Apple or Google?
A: Yes! We support email/password login as well as Sign in with Apple and Google Sign-In for quick and secure authentication.
Q: Do I need to verify my email?
A: Yes, email verification is required for security. Check your inbox for a verification email after signing up. You can resend it from the verification screen if needed.

Troubleshooting

Videos not loading?
  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Close and reopen the app
  3. Clear app cache (Settings → Clear Cache)
  4. Update to the latest version
Login issues?
  1. Verify your email address
  2. Try password reset if you forgot your password
  3. Ensure you're using the correct login method (email vs. social)
  4. Check if your account is verified
Payment or subscription problems?
  1. Check your payment method in App Store/Play Store
  2. Verify subscription status in Settings → Manage Subscription
  3. Contact us with your purchase receipt
